IEDC Startup Bootcamp SJCET


As part of promoting the entrepreneurship culture among students,
the St. Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology, Palai,
in association with the Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM), has decided to set up an Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Center (IEDC Startup BootCamp) at the college. It was inaugurated on the 9th of march, 2015.With the various ideation and problem solving maker events conducted by the Bootcamp, students are encouraged to challenge themselves and come up with innovative ideas that. Students are provided with expert mentorship to convert their ideas into prototypes, and eventually into marketable products.
