About Hackathon


Hackflix is a Hackathon being conducted by Bootcamp of SJCET, Palai with the support of Major Hacking League ( MLH). Hackflix aims at providing provide an opportunity for individual contributors to strengthen soft skills like leadership, mentoring and communication. Hackflix is a risk free platform where team work is encouraged and helps in moulding great interpersonal communication skills.
